About Us

ABOUT US

Enu Soft Drinks Manufacturers is a rapidly expanding soft and carbonated beverages manufacturer formed in Kerala by dedicated entrepreneurs. The team deliberately developed this manufacturing facility based on their worldwide study on sanitary and high-end production techniques that use cutting-edge international technology to serve clients with high-quality carbonated and soft beverages. Our specialised team of scientists and quality control specialists are constantly researching and learning to produce the most fantastic beverages. We are an ISO-recognized brand with FSI accreditation, and our manufacturing facilities use the world's best beverage industry equipment for production purposes.

ENU Drinks is our exclusive product of years of dedication and research to bring various flavours to each drink. We proudly present three major beverages: Squeez, Mozzz, and Popzzz. Goli Soda is a one-of-a-kind product that we created in two different flavours. We have a talented team of marketers, industrialists, and food technologists constantly working towards our aim of introducing truly unique flavours for beverage drink lovers that they have never tasted before.
