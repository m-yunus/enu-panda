about us

Enu Soft Drinks Manufacturers is a rapidly expanding soft and carbonated beverages manufacturer formed in Kerala by dedicated entrepreneurs. The team deliberately developed this manufacturing facility based on their worldwide study on sanitary and high-end production techniques that use cutting-edge international technology to serve clients with high-quality carbonated and soft beverages.

Our specialised team of scientists and quality control specialists are constantly researching and learning to produce the most fantastic beverages. We are an ISO-recognized brand with FSI accreditation, and our manufacturing facilities use the world's best beverage industry equipment for production purposes.
